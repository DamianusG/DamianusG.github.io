ABOUT BLACK ARISTOCRATIC

Black Aristocratic Fine-Art is not only a community for learning, but a platform for contemporary artist whom create critical responses to black art history via their artistic practice. We dedicate ourselves to presenting to you the unvoiced Black noblemen and women through fervent investigation into the archives. Our ultimate objective is to create a holistic space of art history, which unveils unique and rare narratives.
