ABOUT US

Individual freedom and free enterprise are the greatest forces for good ever created in the human sphere. The world has lost sight of that. We use our resources to help bring these ideas back into the light. We extend our counsel, grants, and programs to promote and help like-minded organizations to do the same.


Our goal is to build even more resources and media capacities. We do this to strengthen advocacy for free markets and individual liberty, prevailing in culture as well as in law.

WHO WE ARE

WE ARE A 501(C)3 NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT BELIEVES THAT FREE ENTERPRISE AND LIBERTY UNLEASH THE ESSENCE OF THE HUMAN SPIRIT. CONTRIBUTIONS TO THINK FREELY MEDIA ARE TAX-DEDUCTIBLE TO THE FULLEST EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW.

WHAT WE DO

WE CHAMPION HUMAN FREEDOM AND ACHIEVEMENT TO HELP ALL PEOPLE LIVE FULFILLING, EMPOWERED LIVES.

HOW WE DO IT

We produce content, sponsor tournaments, and provide grants to increase the demand for human freedom and liberty.
